Our history

For many years, Bethany has been baking the most delicius pies at her home.

In 2013, we opened our first store in Brussels (Belgium) in a cosy little street near the Grant Market.

People from all over the world felt in love with the soft cakes, heavenly cheese cakes, spicy apple pies...

The store became a success in no time

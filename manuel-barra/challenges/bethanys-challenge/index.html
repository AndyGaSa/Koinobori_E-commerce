Our History

For many years, Bethany has been baking the most delicious pies at her home. In 2013, we opened our first store in brussels (Belgium) in a cosy litle street near the Grand Market. People from all over the world fell in love with the soft cakes, heavenly cheese cakes, spicy apples pies...

In 2015, no less than 100 pies were sold every day and Bethany's Pie Shop moved into a lager building. After receiving many requests, it became clear that our next step was making it possible to order pies from the confort of your own home and from aniwhere in the world. You are now looking at the result of this: our very own webstore, making it possible to order Bethany's delicious pies whenever you feel like it!
